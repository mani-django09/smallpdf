Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our PDF tools and services

Is SmallPDF.us completely free to use?

Yes, all our essential PDF tools are completely free to use with no hidden fees. We offer premium subscriptions with additional features for professionals and businesses who need enhanced capabilities, higher file size limits, and batch processing.

How secure are my files when using SmallPDF.us?

Your files are protected with military-grade 256-bit SSL encryption during transmission. We automatically delete all uploaded files within hours of processing, and our secure servers prevent unauthorized access. We never share your documents with third parties and are fully GDPR compliant.

What's the maximum file size I can process?

Free users can process PDF files up to 100MB in size. Premium subscribers enjoy higher limits of up to 2GB per file, allowing you to work with large documents, high-resolution designs, and extensive reports. For extremely large files, please contact our support team for custom solutions.

Do I need to create an account to use SmallPDF.us?

No, you can use our basic tools without creating an account. However, registering for a free account gives you benefits like access to your processing history, saved settings, and the ability to upgrade to premium features when needed. Premium accounts include additional features and higher processing limits.

How accurate are your PDF conversions?

Our proprietary conversion technology provides industry-leading accuracy with over 98% formatting retention. We preserve tables, images, fonts, and complex layouts during conversions, ensuring your documents maintain their professional appearance and structure. Our advanced OCR technology accurately extracts text from scanned documents with excellent precision.
